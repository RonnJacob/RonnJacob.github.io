About Me

Welcome, my name is Ronn. I'm a software developer from Kerala, India. Ever since I've taken up computer science as an interest, I've always thought of the domain as a bridge to solutions in various fields. Working as a software developer in the healthcare domain has helped me perceive this thought with much more clarity, by knowing that what I do does make a difference. Along the line, I picked up an interest towards Machine Learning and it's application never cease to amaze me as I continue embarking on the learning journey. I've also got a weak spot for good design, and feel like a user's experience is quite key to truly understanding what the real world expects from us. When I'm not developing or doing anything computer science related, I'm mostly reading up on black holes and space-time articles.
